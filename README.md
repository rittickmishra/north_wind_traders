This project is based on the North Wind Traders dataset, where I performed SQL-based data analysis to extract meaningful business insights. The goal of this project is to practice and demonstrate skills in SQL querying, data analysis, and business problem-solving.

The dataset includes the following tables:
1.Customers
2.Orders
3.Order Details
4.Products
5.Categories
6.Employees
7.Shippers

1. Product Performance
i.Calculated total revenue generated by each product
ii.Identified top 5 best-selling products based on quantity sold
2. Customer Analysis
i.Counted total customers by country
ii.Found customers with more than 10 orders
iii.Identified top 10 customers by total spending
iv.Found customers who ordered the most diverse products
3. Order Analysis
i.Analyzed total orders per year
ii.Calculated running total of freight costs (2014)
iii.Identified late shipments (orders shipped after required date)
4. Shipping & Logistics
i.Determined which shipping company delivered the most orders
5. Employee Hierarchy
i.Analyzed employee-manager relationships using self-joins
